🇸🇬 Singapore — How to Watch

Official broadcaster: Mediacorp. All 104 matches available. 28 matches are free — the opening match, 23 group stage games, both semifinals, 3rd place playoff, and the Final. Everything else requires a paid pass.

🇲🇾 Malaysia — How to Watch

Official broadcasters: RTM and Unifi TV. Astro is NOT broadcasting the World Cup 2026. RTM shows the majority of matches free (some may be delayed). Unifi TV has all 104 live on paid plans. Note: RTM ends its broadcast on Astro from July 1, 2026 — use MyTV or RTMKlik instead.
📺 RTM Free-to-Air TVFREE — No signup
Watch live on your TV via a MyTV decoder (free terrestrial digital broadcast). Scan for channels to get RTM stations. Best for the TV at home.
